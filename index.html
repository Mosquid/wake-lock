Wake Lock

How To Keep Computer From Sleeping When You Are Not Actively Using It

There is a tiny video (~11KB) ↓ playing in the background and preventing your laptop from sleeping.

Just keep this page open in your browser and your laptop stays awake without you touching a keyboard or mouse.

Wake Lock — is a handy feature in the following cases:

  • You want to make sure your uploading/downloading will not get interrupted by your laptop going to sleep.
  • You want to focus on reading a long article on your second screen, and not being annoyed of your laptop screen dims.
  • Any other time-consuming operations that require your laptop to remain on.

Very soon there will be added a bookmarklet allowing you to keep your laptop awake, no matter what site you are on.
